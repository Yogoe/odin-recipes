Buffalo Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 10 chicken wings
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying, or as needed
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 pinch of ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder
